The Song

Kataware Doki (片割れ時) - The ethereal piano piece from Makoto Shinkai's acclaimed anime film "Your Name" (君の名は). Original composition by Yojiro Noda (RADWIMPS), piano arrangement by Theishter.

7-Key Gameplay

Press S, D, F, Space, J, K, L in time with the falling notes. Each lane has colored notes that match the key colors for intuitive play.

How To Play — Rhythm Tap Mechanics

1

Launch & Configure

Click "Start" on the game screen. Before playing, adjust Speed (1x/2x/3x) and optionally enable Challenge Mode (fading notes).

2

Watch the Note Highway

Colored notes fall vertically down 7 lanes. Each lane corresponds to a specific key: S (blue), D (red), F (blue), SPACE (orange), J (blue), K (red), L (blue).

3

Time Your Input

Press the corresponding key exactly when the note reaches the hit zone at the bottom. The game judges your timing accuracy from PERFECT to MISS.

4

Build Your Combo

Chain consecutive GOOD or PERFECT hits to increase your combo counter. Higher combos multiply your score significantly!

5

Complete the Song

The 56-second song plays automatically. Your final score is calculated based on accuracy, combo, and judgment counts. Save your high score by signing in!
