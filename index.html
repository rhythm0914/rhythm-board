How to Play

1

Press START

Click the Start button to begin your rhythm journey. Song loads instantly.

2

Watch the Notes

Colored notes fall down 7 lanes (S, D, F, Space, J, K, L). Match the color to the lane.

3

Tap Perfectly

Press the corresponding key when notes reach the hit zone. The hit window glows on success!

4

Build Your Combo

Chain perfect hits to maximize your score multiplier. Every 40 notes = multiplier up!

🎮 Keys: S D F [SPACE] J K L
🎮 Touch: Tap the on-screen keys
🎮 Pause: Press ESC
⚡ Speed: 1x / 2x / 3x (in-game)
💡 Tip: Use 2 hands for complex patterns 🎯 Focus on the beat, not just visuals 🏆 200+ combo unlocks rainbow trail effect
